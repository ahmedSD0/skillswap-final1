Frequently Asked Questions

Is SkillSwap Local really free?

Yes! Basic membership is completely free. You can post unlimited skills and connect with your community at no cost. We offer a premium membership ($5/month) for additional features like unlimited swaps and priority matching.

How does the time banking work?

Every skill is valued equally. 1 hour of plumbing = 1 hour of graphic design = 1 credit. This creates a fair system where everyone's time and expertise is respected.

Is it safe to meet with strangers?

We recommend meeting in public places for your first few swaps. Check reviews, verify profiles, and always trust your instincts. Premium members get verified badges for extra trust.

What if someone doesn't show up?

You can report no-shows through our platform. Users with multiple reports will be flagged or removed. We take community trust seriously!

Can I swap skills with people far away?

The platform is designed for local exchanges (5-10 miles), but some skills like online tutoring or design work can be done remotely. You decide!

What happens to unused credits?

Your time credits never expire! Save them up and use them whenever you need help. They're your community currency.
